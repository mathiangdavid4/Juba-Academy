About Us

Juba Academy

Providing quality education since 2013

Our Location

Situated along the Nesitu-Nimule Highway, we provide a conducive learning environment with modern facilities.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students academically, socially, and morally for a better future.

Our Community

We foster a supportive community where students, teachers, and parents work together for student success.

Our Programs

Academic Structure

Comprehensive education from S1 to S4

Lower Secondary (S1-S2)

Our Lower Secondary program provides a strong foundation in core subjects while allowing students to explore their interests through electives.

Core Subjects:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • History & Political Ed.
  • Geography
  • Physics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physical Education

Upper Secondary (S3-S4)

Our Upper Secondary program prepares students for national examinations and future academic pursuits with specialized subject combinations.

Core Subjects:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • History & Political Ed.
  • Geography
  • Biology/General Science
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Religious Education
